Big Magic with R - Ingredient 1: Courage

Creative Learning Beyond Fear Last week, I presented a keynote at the Cascadia 2018 R Conference called “Big Magic with R: Creative Learning Beyond Fear”. For this talk, I was inspired by the book “Big Magic: Creative Living Beyond Fear” by Elizabeth Gilbert. In it, Elizabeth argues that there are five essential ingredients needed to live creatively, and they are: courage, enchantment, permission, persistence, and trust. I argued that these same five essential ingredients are needed to creatively learn R, and I talked about why these elements are also essential for advanced users to take their R skills to the next level.
